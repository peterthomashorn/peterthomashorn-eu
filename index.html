Me Elsewhere

After using Facebook and Twitter for several years I drew the conclusion it is an utter waste of time. You invest time and get nothing out of it. And did I already mention how much I hate advertisement? Nearly everybody should be aware of the privacy issues by now, too. Sadly only few people care and are consequent. So in the end I quit. To me it is nothing else than the capitalist counterpart to Chinas mass surveillance.

In the past I used to spend a lot time online and in a purely virtual layer of our society. I am glad I found my way back to focus on life off screen where the actual life happens. I pity those who seem to live in Snapchat and Instagram rather than in tangible reality.

After a few months I occassionally missed that handy little outlet like Twitter. So I finally signed up at Mastodon. For professional reasons I am present on Xing and LinkedIn, too. However I often question the sense of being there. Most interaction is with annoying recruiters contacting me for jobs I do not want. That is the reason why it might take a few weeks until I pick up a message on those channels.
